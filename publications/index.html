Publications

The dg.o conference organizers will invite those authors with high-quality papers to submit their revised versions to journal special issues. Currently, the DGS has cooperated with three journals for publishing special issues after the annual conference:

  • Government Information Quarterly (GIQ)
  • Information Polity
  • International Journal of E-Planning Research (IJEPR)
  • Digital Government: Research and Practice
  • Transforming Government: People, Process, Policy
  • International Journal of E-Government Research
  • International Journal of Public Administration in the Digital Age
