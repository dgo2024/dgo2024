大陸人士簽證

大陸地區簽證已於3月25日團體申請送出，後續將會個別通知辦理情況。

  1. Welcome to all applicants! Please submit the following documents via email:
    1. Acceptance email from the conference.
    2. Proof of conference registration payment.
    3. Application materials.
  2. Submission Deadline: Please email these documents by March 18th.
  3. Visa Fee:
    1. The application fee for an eVisa is NT$1,600. Including the NT$46 processing fee for the online credit card payment, the total amount is NT$1,646.
    2. Citizens of the following countries shall be issued eVisas gratis: Bosnia and Herzegovina, Colombia, Kiribati, Kosovo, Mauritius, Montenegro, Panama, Peru, Solomon Islands, and Turkey.
  4. Once again, thank you to all experts and scholars for your cooperation. Relevant data will be securely destroyed upon obtaining the visa to safeguard personal information. Due to the tight schedule in coordinating visa processes, if you have any outstanding matters or questions, please contact the conference organizing committee. Your understanding is greatly appreciated!
